Grey's Anatomy is a medical show which features many surgeons as they deal with the life in the hospital and personal life. Right now on Netflix, they have 13 seasons. Dear White People is a Netflix Original Show talking about the life of a group of African American college students as they deal with their fellow white classmates. As of right now, the second season is up on Netflix. Criminal Minds is a criminal show which features many cases that the BAU solves. On Netflix there is 12 seasons right now. While it's not on Netflix right now, I suggest that you watch the show Teen Wolf which shows the life a Teen Wolf named Scott and his friends while they deal with the different supernatural forces in Beacon Hills. You can watch the 6 seasons on Amazon Prime. The Originals is a supernatural TV Show of an Original Vampire family as they try to take back their home country, New Orleans. On Netflix there is 4 seasons.
The Harry Potter movies are about Harry Potter as he deal with the mayhem of Voldemort while being in Hogwarts. Get Out is about a black man visiting his white girlfriend's family, where they used Hypnosis on her other black companions. DeadPool is about a mercenary who decides to hurt the guy who ruined his face, and essentially his life.
